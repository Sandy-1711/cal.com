BigBlueButton is an open-source web conferencing system for online learning, meetings, and remote training. It supports real-time sharing of audio, video, slides, chat, and screen, and includes a multi-user whiteboard, breakout rooms, polling, and recording.

To use this integration, you'll need access to a BigBlueButton server. You can [install your own](https://docs.bigbluebutton.org/) or use a hosted provider. After installing the app in Cal.com, enter your server URL and the shared secret (run `bbb-conf --secret` on your BBB server to find it) on the setup page.

Once configured, BigBlueButton appears as a location option on your event types. When a booking is created, Cal.com provisions a meeting room on your BBB server and includes the join link in the confirmation email.
